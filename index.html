Our Services

Booking Flights

As an IATA, ASATA and ANTA accredited travel agency Travis offers cost effective flight reservations to all destinations. Preference for any particular airline is of course taken into consideration whenever reservations are made.

Accomodation

Travis takes pride in giving the customers a choice of their specific accommodation needs, no matter where – regionally, nationally, and internationally.

Visas

Travis has a department which offers professional and timeous visa services. The department also advises clients regarding visa requirements and/or any other information relevant to a specific destination.

Travel insurance

Travis provides travel insurance policies provided by AIG, Hollard and TIC.
